DevOpsDays is making its debut in the Rocky Mountains on Tuesday, July 22nd and Wednesday, July 23rd.
The event will be held in downtown Denver (location to be announced soon!) and promises two days of learning, networking and fun. With a mixture of traditional sessions, keynotes, Ignite talks and an open space format, there are many opportunities to get involved… especially if you’re a new speaker or are presenting a talk for the first time.
Check back soon for more information on submitting a talk, registering and sponsoring.
